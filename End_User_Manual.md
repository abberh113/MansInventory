# Mans Inventory System - End User Manual

## Welcome to Mans Inventory System
This system enables efficient management of products, orders, categories, system users, and general day-to-day administrative features. Based on your access level (Super Admin, HR, Admin, Staff), some features may be hidden from you.

## System Features and How to Use Them

### 1. Authentication and Logging In
- **To Login:** Access the application landing page. Click 'Login', and provide your registered `Email` and `Password`.
- **Forgot Password:** In case of forgotten credentials, use the `Forgot Password` link on the login page to securely reset your credentials.

### 2. Dashboard Interface
Upon a successful login, you'll be greeted by the Dashboard. 
- The Dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your business operations: Total Orders, recent movements, stock depletion levels, and financial performance overviews.

### 3. Product Management (Products Page)
- **Viewing Products:** You can browse the table of available inventory. Images, Stock Quantities, Categories, and Unit Prices are listed here.
- **Adding a Product:** Click "Add Product" and enter the details. You will need to input a unique SKU, price, initial stock level, and category.
- **Updating Stock:** Whenever new inventory arrives, edit a product to update its quantity on hand.

### 4. Categories Organizing (Categories Page)
- If you sell different classes of products, the Categories section helps group them.
- To create a category, go to the Categories page and provide a Name (e.g., Electronics, Consumables) and an optional description. Every product must be tagged to an existing category.

### 5. Managing Sales and Orders (Orders Page & Receipts)
- **Creating Sales (Checkout):** Use the interface to pick items requested by a customer. The unit prices will automatically compile to calculate a Total.
- **Payment Method:** The system accepts various payment entries: Cash, Transfer, Card, or POS. Make sure to specify this before confirming the order.
- **Status Processing:** New orders typically begin as 'Pending' and can be upgraded to 'Completed' or marked 'Cancelled' for aborted checkouts. 
- **Generating Receipts:** After an order completes, you can proceed to print or save a generated receipt for the customer from the connected Receipt view.

### 6. User Management (HR & Admins Only)
- Located via the **Users Page**, authorized Admins and HR personnel can onboard new staff.
- New accounts are mandated to have a specific role assigned (e.g., normal_staff, branch_admin).
- You can activate or deactivate accounts if a staff member goes on leave or leaves the company.

### 7. Audit System & Transparency
- If you have adequate administrative privileges, the **Audit Logs** track significant system changes. Every creation, update, order placement, or user configuration is saved against the user's name, timestamp, and IP Address for complete system integrity.

## Support & Troubleshooting
If you encounter access problems or system errors:
1. Ensure your network connection is active.
2. Confirm with HR/Super Admin that your account is set to "Active".
3. Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
